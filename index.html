Safety Tips

1. Never share your OTP, PIN, or password with anyone — even bank officials.
2. Do NOT scan unknown QR codes. Verify the payee name before confirming.
3. Ignore messages claiming you won a lottery or prize.
4. Do not click links from unknown numbers. Go directly to official websites.
5. Verify video calls — deepfake videos can fake a person's face and voice.
6. Report fraud: Call 1930 (Cyber Crime Helpline, India).
